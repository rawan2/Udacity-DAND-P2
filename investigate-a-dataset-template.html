Conclusions¶

At the end the dataset was almost clean, And I found that the largest percentage is for patients who did not show up in the appointment and also for the patients did not receive the SMS, addition for the diseases I found that the Hipertension got the highest value , due to these founds I suggest the following solutions :
1- send SMS to all patients .
2- Educating the community about the dangers of Hipertension disease and how to prevent it.
3- put a financial penalty for not attending the appointment :) .
